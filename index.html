RESUME

— I am an Austrian-American actor, filmmaker, politician, and former professional bodybuilder and powerlifter. I had many dreams in my life and I made them true. I realized my dream of becoming the best body-builder in the world when won the Mr Olympia title. I dreamt of becoming an actor, and tmy dream too was fulfilled as I went on to become a big Hollywood star. I was politically active and later on served as the Governor of California for two terms. I also have a philanthropic side and help many charitable organizations in their cause. After completing my second term as governor, I have retired from politics and am currently pursuing my acting career.

EXPERIENCE

  • 1967

    Earned the «Mr. Universe» title when was only twenty years old, thus, becoming the youngest individual to win the title. The following year, again won the Mr. Universe title.

  • 1970

    • Competed in the Mr. Olympia contest and won the contest when I was only twenty-three years old. I created a record of being the youngest person to earn the title.
    • Stepped into Hollywood and began my career with the film «Hercules in New York», and then, I worked in the film «The Long Goodbye».

  • 1971-1975

    Emerged as the winner of Mr. Olympia contest and in 1975, declared my intention to withdraw from professional body-building.

  • 1976

    My film «Stay Hungry» was released in which my acting got appreciated and I also won an award for my film.

  • 1977

    Worked in the film «Pumping Iron», also featured in an episode of the comedy show «The San Pedro Beach Bums».

  • 1979

    Acted in the film «The Villain», and the following year, appeared in the biographical movie «The Jayne Mansfield Story», as actress Jayne Mansfield’s husband Mickey Hargitay.

  • 1980

    Participated in the Mr. Olympia competition and won it as well. Finally, officially took retirement from professional body-building.

  • 1982

    • Got my first major hit when my film «Conan the Barbarian» was released.
    • Worked on the sequel of tmy film called «Conan the Destroyer». This was followed by many more films such as «The Terminator», «Predator», «Commando», «The Running Man», «Raw Deal», Red Heat» which launched me as a successful action hero.
    • Did a comic role in the 1988 film «Twins».

  • 1990-1993

    Chaired the «President»s Council on Physical Fitness and Sports» and was then the chairperson of the «California Governor»s Council on Physical Fitness and Sports». I was also the ambassador of the «Red Cross» which is an international humanitarian movement.

  • 1991

    Played the protagonist in the «Terminator 2: Judgement Day» which was immensely successful and became the biggest commercial hit of that year.

  • 1994

    Worked in the film «True Lies», and the same year, in the film «Junior» which earned me a «Golden Globe Award» nomination.

  • 2002

    Acted in the movie «The 6th Day» and then, starred in «Collateral Damage» but none of these films were met with financial success.

  • 2003

    • Played the lead role in the film «Terminator 3: Rise of the Machines», and it marked this actor’s comeback into stardom as it was a huge hit and garnered around $150 million in the US
    • Declared to contest the «2003 California recall» election for the office of the Governor of California. I was elected as the Governor of California on 7th October, the same year and remained in office for the next eight years, which included a re-election to the post.

  • 2004

    Made a guest appearance in the film «The Rundown» and then another cameo role in «Around the World in 80 Days».

  • 2011

    • After completing my second term as the Governor of California, I decided to make a comeback in Hollywood.
    • Founded the NGO named «R20 Regions of Climate Action».

  • 2012-1014

  • 2012

  • 2015
